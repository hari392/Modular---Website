Journal Voucher


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to add a Journal Voucher and enter the necessary details such as branch names, product names, customer names, account IDs, GL codes, transaction amounts, and remarks. Journal Voucher is used to update the General Ledger in the books of accounts of the transactions that has taken place. Following these steps will ensure accurate and organized record-keeping for financial transactions.
