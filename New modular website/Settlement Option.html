Settlement Option


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to add a settlement option in Uncia Studio. Settlement Option is the way in which financial transactions are settled. In SCF there are two Settlement Options, i.e., Receipt and Payment. The Payment type of settlement refers to the repayment transaction done to the FI by a customer. In the case of Receipt type, the settlement refers to disbursement done by the FI to its customer.


Raghuram | 9 steps | 2 minutes


Click on Resume.

Click "+ Add Another" to add a Settlement.

Select the appropriate value from the dropdown.

Choose whether the settlement option is Payment or Receipt.

Choose the appropriate GL Codes from the List of Values.

Click on Branch to select from the list of branches.

Select the Branch from the list.

Enter the Account Number.

click "Submit"
