Charge Collection - Charges


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to add and submit charge collections, including selecting payment modes, banks, customers, and charge codes. It also covers entering the received amount, reference number, allocated amount, and narration. Charge Collection is a process that helps in the collection of certain charges from customers. Viewing this guide will help users navigate the process efficiently and accurately.


Sudhanshu Chandra | 19 steps | 2 minutes


Click to add New Charge Collection.

Click to view the list of Mode of Payments to select.

Select the payment mode from the list.

Click to view the list of Bank Names to select.

Select the Bank from the list.

Click to view the list of Customer Name to select.

7 Select the Customer from the list.

Select the Value Date.
Value Date is the date on which the transaction is taking place. Value date will define the date on which the Charge collected from the customer.

Click on "Continue" to move on to enter Charge Collection details.

Enter the amount received.

Enter the Reference No.
Reference Number is not system generated and has to be entered. This is relevant for the Financial Institution's records of Charge Collection.

Click to view the list of Charge Codes to select.

Select the Charge from the list.

Click on "+ Add".

Click to view the list of Charge Names to select.

Enter the Charge Allocated Amount(With GST).

Enter the Narration.

Click to Submit the Charge Collection.

Click on Submit.
