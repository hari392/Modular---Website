Refund Processing


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to process a refund, including selecting the customer, inputting the payment amount, choosing the payment method, and selecting the bank details. Refund Processing helps to refund the excess money to a customer. Following these steps will ensure a smooth and accurate refund process.


Raghuram | 10 steps | 44 seconds


Click on 'Refund Processing' to add a Refund Details.

Select the Customer Name

Select the customer from the list.

Input the Payment Amount.

Select the appropriate mode of Payment.

Click on the Bank Payment Details.

Select the Bank Details from the list.

Click on the Account Number of the Beneficiary.

Select the Bank Account from the list.

Select Submit
