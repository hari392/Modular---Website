NDC/NOC


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to set up limits for a customer within a financial institution. Limits are the sanctioned amount given by the financial institution to the organization. It covers creating limits, entering necessary details such as limit amount and expiry date, and creating sub limits if needed. It also explains the different types of limits and product variants available.
