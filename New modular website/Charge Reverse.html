Charge Reverse


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to initiate Charge Reverse. Charge Reverse, is done by a FI when it wants to waive-off a charge collected from a customer. It includes details on creating a charge reverse, adding charge reverse details and submitting the charge reverse for approval. Following this guide will ensure a smooth and accurate initiation of charge reverse.


Raghuram | 8 steps | 29 seconds


Click on the New Charge Reverse button for initiating a charge reverse.

Select Customer Name.

Select the applicable customer name from the list.

Select the Charge Reverse Type from the dropdown list.

Click on Continue to go to the Charge Reverse Details.

Click on "+ Add" for adding the charge details.

Select Charge Name.
Enter Charge Date and Charge Due Amount.

Click on save for processing charge reverse..
