Direct Excess Cancel


This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to cancel a direct excess payment. It outlines the necessary information and actions required to successfully complete the cancellation process. By following these steps, users can efficiently and effectively cancel direct excess payments, ensuring accurate financial records and customer satisfaction.


Raghuram | 3 steps | 15 seconds


Click on "Direct Excess Cancel" button for cancelling a direct excess.

Select the Direct Excess Batch ID.
Enter mode of payment, bank name, customer name and excess amount.

Click on continue to go to the Direct Excess Cancel Details.
